About Me

Thank you for checking this page out.  Get to know a little more about me.

Someone asked me once why I enjoy jigsaw puzzles so much.  At the time, I didnt have an answer.  Twenty years later, I now know.  I enjoy creating order from chaos.  How fitting for Data Analysis.  You start with an idea of what the end picture looks like and work your way there.  Searching, sorting, matching, and fitting the pieces together.  Eventually, the story you are trying to tell comes together and the picture become more and more clear.  This is where my passion lies.  

Being known as "The Data Guy", I would always help my peers at work when it came to anything that had to do with numbers or a spreadsheet.  This eventually grew into dashboards and visualization.  I was enjoying it so much, I made the decision that it was time to explore turning my passion into a career.

I enjoy going tent camping every year, especially in fall when the weather is cooling and the leaves are starting to change color.  Northern Wisconsin is my favorite area to go.  Rural areas lead to relaxing retreats along side clear waterways, tall pines, and the sounds of nature.  For as much as I enjoy sitting behind the computer screen working on projects, sometimes it is nice to unplug and spend some time away in the outdoors.

When the weather is decent, I can usually be found outside with my pride and joy dogs; Lady and Deeks.  Both Shepherd / Husky mixes with polar opposite personalities.  Lady was a shy rescue that came from a hoarder who had 40 dogs on a small property in Mississippi.  Deeks was picked up in Texas and looks like a German Shepherd with all of the attitude of a Husky.

In the colder months, I can usually be found working on indoor hobbies.  I enjoy reading a good non-fiction book, playing a video game, or talking with someone from around the world on one of my amateur radios in the basement.
